## 1. Information we collect

### 1.1 Personal information

When you create an account via Google Authentication, we collect **only**:

- Name
- Email address

We explicitly do **not** collect phone numbers, physical addresses, passwords, or any other personal identifiers.

### 1.2 Financial data (card selection)

We do not collect, store, or process sensitive financial information such as Credit Card Numbers (PAN), CVV/CVC codes, PINs, or One-Time Passwords (OTPs).

- **Card Management:** When you add a card to your portfolio, we only store the Bank Name and Card Name you select from our predefined list.
- **No Verification:** We do not verify card ownership or link to your actual bank accounts.

### 1.3 Usage data

We may collect information on how the Service is accessed and used to improve performance. This includes your device's IP address, browser type, pages visited, time spent on pages, and diagnostic data.

### 1.4 Advertising data

Our app integrates Google Ads. Google may collect:

- Device identifiers (e.g., Advertising ID)
- Interaction data (e.g., clicks, impressions)
- General demographic/interest data based on app usage

## 2. How we use your information

We use the limited information we collect strictly for the following purposes:

| Purpose | Details |
|---|---|
| **Service Provision** | To power the credit card rewards calculator and card management tools |
| **Improvement** | To analyze usage patterns and optimize the Service |
| **Communication** | To contact you about critical service updates or support requests — not marketing |
| **Security** | To detect, prevent, and address technical issues or fraud |
| **Advertising** | To serve relevant advertisements through Google Ads, supporting the free availability of the Service |

## 3. Data storage and processing

**Secure Storage:** Your account profile (Name, Email) and card portfolio (list of card names) are stored in **Firebase**, a Google Cloud service. We rely on Google's industry-leading security measures to protect this data.

**Computation (No Retention):** All reward calculations, MCC lookups, and transfer ratio computations are processed on **Cloudflare's** global infrastructure in real-time. The specific transaction details you enter for calculation are not permanently stored.

**Processing Locations:** User data is stored securely in Firebase while calculations utilize Cloudflare's global network to ensure fast, low-latency service delivery.

## 6. Accuracy of information

- Reward calculations are based on publicly available data and are for reference only.
- Banks may change their reward terms without notice. The bank's official terms are always final and binding.
- Users are responsible for verifying calculations with their bank before making financial decisions.

## 7. Children's privacy

Our Service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.
