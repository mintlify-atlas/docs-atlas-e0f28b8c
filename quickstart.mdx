This guide walks you through opening CCReward, picking your region, signing in, running your first reward calculation, and saving a card to your collection.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open CCReward">
    Navigate to [ccreward.app](https://ccreward.app) in any browser. The app works on desktop and mobile — no installation required.

    <Tip>
      CCReward is also available as a native app. Download it from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/in/app/ccreward/id6736835206) or [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=app.ccreward) for the best mobile experience.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Choose your region">
    On your first visit, CCReward detects your location automatically. If you are browsing from **India** or **Singapore**, your region is set for you and you can move on to the next step.

    If your location cannot be detected, a **Region Selection** modal appears. Choose the region that matches the cards you hold:

    - **India (IN)** — covers AMEX, AU, Axis, BOB, Canara, DBS, Equitas, Federal, HDFC, HSBC, ICICI, IDBI, IDFCFirst, IndusInd, Kotak, OneCard, PNB, RBL, SBI, SBM, SC, Union, YesBank and more.
    - **Singapore (SG)** — covers AMEX, BOC, CIMB, Citi, DBS, DCS, GXS, HSBC, Maribank, Maybank, OCBC, POSB, SC, TrustBank, UOB and more.

    <Note>
      You can change your region at any time from the header. Switching regions reloads the bank and card lists for the new country.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Sign in">
    CCReward supports two sign-in options:

    <Tabs>
      <Tab title="Sign in with Google">
        Click **Sign in with Google** and complete the Google OAuth flow in the popup. Your card collection and calculation history are saved to your account.
      </Tab>
      <Tab title="Sign in with Apple">
        Click **Sign in with Apple** and authenticate with your Apple ID in the popup. Your data is tied to your Apple account.
      </Tab>
    </Tabs>

    <Note>
      Sign-in is optional for the reward calculator. You can run calculations without an account, but saving cards to **My Cards** requires signing in.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Run your first reward calculation">
    Open the **Reward Calculator** from the main navigation. Fill in the form fields in order:

    1. **Select a bank** — choose the issuing bank for your card (e.g. HDFC, DBS).
    2. **Select a card** — the card dropdown populates once a bank is chosen.
    3. **Search MCC** *(optional)* — type a merchant name, category, or 4-digit MCC code to narrow the rewards to a specific spend category. Results show the MCC code, category name, and known merchants. Type at least 2 characters to trigger the search.
    4. **Enter the amount** — type the transaction amount. Use the currency selector to switch between local and foreign currencies for international spend.
    5. **Answer card-specific questions** *(if shown)* — some cards ask additional questions (e.g. card tier, spend milestone) to calculate the correct reward tier. These appear automatically after you select the card.

    Click **Calculate** to see your results.

    <Tip>
      Not sure which MCC applies to a merchant? Search by the merchant's name — CCReward maps known merchants to their MCC codes so you don't need to look them up manually.
    </Tip>

    <Warning>
      The **Calculate** button stays disabled until a bank, card, and a positive amount are all entered. If the button remains greyed out, check that the amount field is not zero or empty.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Understand the results">
    After clicking Calculate, a results panel appears below the form:

    - **Green panel** — your card earns rewards for this spend. The panel shows the reward text (e.g. "500 points" or "₹120 cashback" or "1.2 miles").
    - **Red panel** — no rewards are earned for this combination of card and merchant category.

    If your transaction is in a foreign currency, a currency conversion note is shown below the reward text, explaining the exchange rate used.

    <Note>
      Reward calculations use real-time exchange rates for foreign currency transactions. If exchange rates are temporarily unavailable, retry after a few minutes or switch to your local currency.
    </Note>

    After you receive results, two optional actions appear:

    - **Bank or Card Missing?** — report a missing bank or card so it can be added.
    - **Report Incorrect Reward** — flag a calculation that looks wrong, pre-filled with your current inputs.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add a card to My Cards">
    Once a calculation has been performed, an **Add to My Cards** button appears below the results (visible only when you are signed in). Click it to save the bank and card to your personal collection.

    Your saved cards appear in the **My Cards** section, where you can manage your full card portfolio.

    <Note>
      If you are not signed in, the button reads **Sign In & Add to My Cards**. Clicking it takes you through the sign-in flow before adding the card.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>
