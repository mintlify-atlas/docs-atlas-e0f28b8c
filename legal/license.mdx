## Commercial license

For any commercial use of the Software — including but not limited to using it as part of a business process, incorporating it into a commercial product or service, or any use intended for commercial advantage or monetary compensation — a **separate commercial license is required**.

Licensing proposals are handled over email for companies of all sizes:

| Company size | Process |
|---|---|
| Small (1–10 employees) | Formal proposal over email |
| Medium (11–250 employees) | Formal proposal over email |
| Large (251+ employees) | Formal proposal over email |

### How to request a commercial license

Contact the team at [support@ccreward.app](mailto:support@ccreward.app) with:

- Your company name and size
- A description of how you intend to use CCReward
- Any specific requirements or questions about the commercial terms

You'll receive a formal proposal based on the scope of use.

## Attribution requirements

Regardless of whether you use the project under the open source or commercial license, **attribution is required** if you use, modify, or distribute the project.

Your attribution must include:

1. **Project name:** ccreward.app
2. **GitHub repository URL:** https://github.com/aashishvanand/ccreward-web
3. **Original author:** Aashish Vivekanand

Example attribution statement:

```
This project is based on ccreward (https://github.com/aashishvanand/ccreward-web) by Aashish Vivekanand.
```
