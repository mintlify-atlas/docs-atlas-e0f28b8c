My Cards is your personal credit card portfolio within CCReward. You can add every card you hold, and the app uses your portfolio to power features like the [Best Card Finder](/features/best-card-finder) — which compares all your cards at once for any transaction.

<Warning>
  My Cards requires you to be **signed in**. Your portfolio is tied to your account and is not available to unauthenticated users.
</Warning>

## What you can do in My Cards

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Add cards" icon="plus">
    Add any credit card from a supported bank to your portfolio. Cards are region-aware — your list shows cards available in India or Singapore based on your active region.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Remove cards" icon="trash">
    Delete a card from your portfolio at any time. This also removes it from Best Card Finder comparisons.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Edit card details" icon="pencil">
    Update optional details such as card network (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, RuPay) after adding a card.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Share your portfolio" icon="share">
    Generate a shareable image of your card collection to share with friends or on social media.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Adding a card

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Add Card dialog">
    Tap the **+** floating action button (speed dial) in the bottom-right corner of the My Cards page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your bank">
    Search for and select your card's issuing bank. Start typing to filter the list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your card">
    Once a bank is selected, choose your specific card from the list. Only cards supported for your active region are shown.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a card network (optional)">
    Optionally select the card network — Visa, Mastercard, Amex, or RuPay. This is optional and does not affect reward calculations.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap Add Card">
    Confirm to add the card. It will appear in your portfolio immediately.

    <Note>
      If you try to add a card that already exists in your portfolio, you will see an "already in your collection" message and the duplicate will not be added.
    </Note>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## How My Cards integrates with the calculator

Your saved cards work with other features:

- **Best Card Finder** — automatically loads all cards from your portfolio and compares them simultaneously for any transaction. You need at least 2 cards for this to work.
- **Reward Calculator** — after running a calculation, an **Add to My Cards** button appears (when signed in) so you can quickly save the card you just evaluated.

<Tip>
  Add at least 2 cards to unlock the Best Card Finder. The app will prompt you to add a second card if your portfolio only has one.
</Tip>

## Managing your collection

Each card in your portfolio can be edited or deleted by interacting with it in the card list. Changes sync to your account immediately.

When you switch regions (India ↔ Singapore), the portfolio view refreshes automatically to show cards relevant to the new region.
