The Reward Calculator lets you select a specific credit card, enter a spend amount, and get a precise breakdown of the points, cashback, or miles you will earn — including their equivalent value in INR or SGD.

It accounts for Merchant Category Codes (MCCs), foreign currency spends, and card-specific conditions such as bonus categories and spend thresholds.

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select a bank">
    Choose your card's issuing bank from the dropdown. The list is filtered to your active region (India or Singapore).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a card">
    Pick the specific credit card from the list of cards available for that bank.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for an MCC (optional but recommended)">
    Type at least 2 characters in the MCC search field to find a Merchant Category Code by merchant name, category name, or known merchant. For example, typing "swiggy" or "dining" will surface the relevant category.

    Selecting an MCC allows the calculator to apply the correct reward rate for that spending category. Without an MCC, the calculator uses the card's base reward rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the spend amount and currency">
    Type the amount you spent. You can also change the currency if you made a foreign currency transaction — the calculator will apply the relevant cross-currency or foreign transaction rate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer card-specific questions">
    Some cards have additional conditions that affect reward calculation — for example, whether you've met a monthly spend threshold, or whether the transaction is online or in-store. Answer any questions that appear for your selected card.

    <Note>
      Wait for the questions to finish loading before clicking Calculate.
    </Note>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Calculate">
    Press the **Calculate** button to see your reward results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding your results

The result panel shows a plain-language summary of what you earn. Depending on the card, this can include:

- **Points** — reward points credited to your card account
- **Cashback** — direct cashback applied to your statement
- **Miles** — air miles (for co-branded or travel cards)
- **Equivalent value** — the estimated INR or SGD value of the reward

A green result panel means you earned rewards. A red panel with "No rewards earned" means the transaction category is excluded for that card.

If you spent in a foreign currency, the calculator also shows the currency conversion details that were applied.

## What are MCCs and why do they matter?

A **Merchant Category Code (MCC)** is a 4-digit code assigned by card networks to classify businesses by the type of goods or services they sell. Banks use these codes to determine which reward rate applies to a transaction.

For example:
- A transaction at a grocery store gets a different MCC than one at a fuel station.
- Many premium cards offer **2x or 3x bonus points** on specific MCCs such as dining, travel, or online shopping — but only **base rewards** on others.
- Some categories (like rent payments, wallet loads, or fuel surcharges) may be **excluded from rewards entirely**.

Selecting the correct MCC ensures the calculator applies the right multiplier for your card.

<Tip>
  Use the [MCC Lookup](/features/mcc-lookup) tool to identify the correct code for any merchant before running a calculation.
</Tip>

## Usage limits

<Info>
  The Reward Calculator is rate-limited for users who are not signed in. Free-tier users (including signed-in users on the web) get **10 calculations per day**. The counter is shown in the top-right of the calculator panel when you are signed in.

  If you reach your daily limit, sign in or use the CCReward mobile app for higher limits.
</Info>

## Reporting issues

After a calculation, two report buttons appear:

- **Missing bank or card** — if your bank or card is not listed
- **Incorrect reward** — if the result looks wrong for your card

Submitting a report helps the team keep reward data accurate.
