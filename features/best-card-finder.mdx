---
title: Best Card Finder
description: Compare all the cards in your portfolio at once to find which one earns the most rewards for a given transaction.
---

The Best Card Finder calculates rewards across every card in your [My Cards](/features/my-cards) portfolio simultaneously and ranks them for a specific transaction. Instead of checking each card one by one, you get a ranked list showing which card earns the most — sorted by points, value, or miles.

<Note>
  You need at least **2 cards** in your My Cards portfolio to use this feature. Sign in and add your cards first.
</Note>

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in and add your cards">
    The Best Card Finder uses your saved card portfolio. Make sure you have signed in and added at least 2 cards via [My Cards](/features/my-cards).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for an MCC (optional)">
    Type a merchant name or category in the MCC search field to narrow the reward calculation to that category. Without an MCC, the base reward rates for each card are used.

    Type at least 2 characters to trigger the search (e.g. "amazon", "flight", "groceries").
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the spend amount and currency">
    Enter how much you plan to spend. Change the currency if it is a foreign currency transaction.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use Advanced Mode for card-specific conditions (optional)">
    Expand the **Advanced Mode** section to answer card-specific questions for individual cards in your portfolio. This is useful when certain cards offer bonus rewards only under specific conditions (e.g. minimum monthly spend met, online vs. in-store).

    Questions only appear for cards where they are relevant, and are further filtered by the selected MCC.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Calculate Best Card">
    Press **Calculate Best Card**. The calculator sends your entire portfolio to the engine and returns a ranked result list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the ranked results">
    Cards are ranked from best to worst reward. The top card is highlighted in green. Use the sort toggle to switch between views:

    - **Ranking by Points/Cashback** — sorted by raw points or cashback earned
    - **Ranking by Value (₹ or S$)** — sorted by the estimated monetary value of the reward
    - **Ranking by Miles** — sorted by miles earned
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Interpreting the results

Each card in the ranked list shows:

- **Rank and card name** — e.g. "1. HDFC – Infinia"
- **Reward summary** — a plain-language description of what you earn (e.g. "500 Reward Points worth ₹200")

The **#1 card** (top of the list) is the best choice for that specific transaction under the conditions you entered.

<Tip>
  Switch between the three sort modes to see if a different card wins for value or miles, even if it doesn't earn the most raw points.
</Tip>

If a foreign currency is involved, the conversion details used for the calculation are shown below the sort toggle.

## Usage limits

<Info>
  The Best Card Finder is rate-limited. Free-tier users get **10 calculations per day**. The counter appears in the top-right of the calculator panel when signed in.
</Info>
