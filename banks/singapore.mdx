## How bank and card data is fetched

The list of supported banks for Singapore is loaded dynamically from the CCReward API:

```
GET /v4/bank?country=sg
```

Once a bank is selected, its available credit cards are fetched separately:

```
GET /v4/card?bank={bankName}&country=sg
```

Both responses are cached in `localStorage` for 24 hours to minimise API calls on repeated visits.

<Info>
  The card list is always fetched dynamically. There is no static list embedded in the app — new cards added to the CCReward database become available to all users automatically.
</Info>

## Singapore reward types

Singapore credit cards offer several reward structures. Many premium and travel cards in Singapore are **miles-focused**, where spend earns air miles that can be transferred to frequent flyer programmes.

<Note>
  Singapore cards commonly offer one of three reward types:

  - **Miles** — earned as air miles, often transferable to KrisFlyer, Asia Miles, or other FFPs. Popular on DBS Altitude, OCBC 90°N, Citi PremierMiles, and UOB PRVI Miles.
  - **Points** — proprietary bank points (e.g. DBS Points, OCBC$, UNI$) redeemable for vouchers, miles transfers, or merchandise.
  - **Cashback** — a percentage of spend returned as statement credit or cash. Common on OCBC 365, DBS Live Fresh, and Maybank Family & Friends.

  CCReward calculates the optimal card for each spend category regardless of reward type.
</Note>
