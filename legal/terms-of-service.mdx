## 2. Description of service

CCReward is a comprehensive credit card rewards calculator and management tool for users in **India** and **Singapore**. The Service allows users to:

- Calculate reward points for specific transactions
- Identify the best card to use for a given transaction to maximize returns
- Perform Merchant Category Code (MCC) lookups
- Calculate and compare reward transfer ratios to partner airlines and hotels
- Manage a portfolio of credit cards for tracking purposes
