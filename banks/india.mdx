## How bank and card data is fetched

The list of supported banks for India is loaded dynamically from the CCReward API:

```
GET /v4/bank?country=in
```

Once a bank is selected, its available credit cards are fetched separately:

```
GET /v4/card?bank={bankName}&country=in
```

Both responses are cached in `localStorage` for 24 hours to minimise API calls on repeated visits.

<Info>
  The card list is always fetched dynamically. There is no static list embedded in the app — new cards added to the CCReward database become available to all users automatically.
</Info>

## MCC-based rewards and exclusion lists

Indian credit cards calculate rewards based on the **Merchant Category Code (MCC)** of the transaction. Most banks maintain exclusion lists — specific MCC categories that earn reduced rewards or no rewards at all.

<Note>
  Common exclusions across Indian banks include fuel transactions, utility bill payments, government services, insurance premiums, EMI conversions, and wallet top-ups. The exact exclusion list varies by card. CCReward accounts for these exclusions in every calculation.
</Note>

When you select an MCC in the calculator, CCReward automatically checks the card's exclusion list and adjusts the reward output accordingly.

## Missing bank or card?

<Tip>
  If you can't find your bank or credit card, use the **"Bank or Card Missing?"** feature inside the app to submit a request. The CCReward team reviews submissions and adds verified cards to the database.
</Tip>
