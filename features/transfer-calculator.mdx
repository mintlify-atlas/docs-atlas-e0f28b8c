The Transfer Calculator shows you how your credit card reward points can be converted into airline miles or hotel loyalty points via transfer partnerships. For each partner, it shows the points you will receive, the estimated monetary value, and the typical transfer processing time.

Below the calculator, a **Transfer Partners Matrix** gives an at-a-glance view of which banks support which airline and hotel programs.

<Note>
  The Transfer Calculator requires you to be **signed in**. If you are not authenticated, the Calculate button is disabled.
</Note>

## What are transfer partners?

Many credit cards earn points in a proprietary rewards currency (e.g. HDFC Reward Points, Amex Membership Rewards, DBS Points). Banks allow you to **transfer** these points to external loyalty programs at a fixed ratio.

Transfer partners fall into two categories:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Airline programs" icon="plane">
    Miles credited to an airline's frequent flyer program. Useful for booking award flights, upgrades, and companion tickets. Examples include Air India Flying Returns, Singapore Airlines KrisFlyer, and IndiGo BluChip.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Hotel programs" icon="building">
    Points credited to a hotel loyalty program. Used for free nights, room upgrades, and dining credits. Examples include Marriott Bonvoy, IHG One Rewards, and Hilton Honors.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

The **transfer ratio** determines how many partner points you receive per card point transferred. Ratios vary by card and program — for example, 2:1 means 2 card points become 1 airline mile.

## How to use it

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign in">
    Sign in to your CCReward account. The calculator is only available to authenticated users.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a bank">
    Choose the issuing bank of the card whose points you want to transfer.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select a card">
    Pick the specific card. Only cards with a transferable points currency will return results. If your card does not support transfers, the calculator will inform you.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter the number of points to transfer">
    Type the number of points you want to convert. Use the clear button (×) to reset the field.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Calculate Transfers">
    Press **Calculate Transfers**. Results appear grouped into Airlines and Hotels sections.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Understanding the results

After calculating, each transfer partner entry shows:

- **Program name and brand** — e.g. "Air India (Flying Returns)"
- **Points received** — the number of loyalty points or miles you will receive after transfer
- **Estimated value** — the estimated monetary value (in INR or SGD) of those points
- **Value per point** — how much each transferred point is worth in your local currency
- **Transfer time** — the typical processing time for the transfer to reflect in your loyalty account

<Tip>
  Compare the **value per point** across partners to identify the most rewarding transfer option for your goals.
</Tip>

## Transfer Partners Matrix

Below the calculator is the **Transfer Partners Matrix** — a reference table listing all supported transfer partners and which banks have a relationship with each program. Toggle between the Airlines and Hotels tabs, and hover over a checkmark to see the points currency involved.
